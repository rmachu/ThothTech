## About Us

<Card title="Thoth Tech Mission">

At Thoth Tech, we create tools designed to enhance educational outcomes by:

- Empowering students
- Connecting them with tutors
- Facilitating personalised learning experiences

### Our Values

Our values define how we operate, what we represent, and guide us to be the kind of company and team
members we aspire to be. By living up to these values, we aim to:

#### Be People-Focused

- **Expect the Best:** We hold each other to high standards and provide constructive feedback.
- **Encourage Growth:** We support each other in taking initiative to improve both personally and
  professionally.
- **Collaborate with Kindness:** We work together respectfully and with kindness.

#### Uphold Sustainable Excellence

- **Create Quality Software:** We develop software that is not only functional but also maintainable
  and enjoyable to use.
- **Ensure Sustainability:** We strive to work in a way that is sustainable for our team and the
  environment.

#### Be Inclusive and Supportive

- **Celebrate Diversity:** We value diverse perspectives and embrace challenging ideas and
  conversations.
- **Foster Inclusivity:** We support each other and encourage an environment where everyone feels
  included.

By adhering to these principles, we aim to build a collaborative, innovative, and respectful
workplace.
